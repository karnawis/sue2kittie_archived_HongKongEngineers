Message from Kam Ho, February 2011

Dear All,

Kung Hei Fat Choy!

Thanks for browsing HKIECC’s website and supporting our activities over the years. Time flies. I wish everyone prosperous, happy and healthy in the Year of Rabbit and enjoy your upcoming Family Day.

We plan the Chinese New Year dinner at Starwalk Buffet at Scarborough/Markham www.starwalkbuffet.com on Thursday February 24, 2011. Let’s mingle, relax and exchange information (since we met August last year) during this special moment with one another in HKIECC family. I look forward to seeing you.

Yours Sincerely

Kam Ho
